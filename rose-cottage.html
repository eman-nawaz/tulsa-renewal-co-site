rosé cottage

Rosé Cottage is a reimagined charming 1920's home! We thoughtfully renovated the cottage to celebrate its vintage character while adding a touch of modern whimsy. The standout feature is a one-of-a-kind pink kitchen, blending playful color with retro-inspired design for a fresh yet nostalgic feel. original woodwork has been carefully preserved and restored, showcasing the craftsmanship of the era. Mosaic windows add a splash of color and artistry, casting warm light across the rooms.

Every detail was chosen to honor the home's history while giving it new life, resulting in a space that feels both timeless and full of personality. Scroll through the photos to see the transformation!
